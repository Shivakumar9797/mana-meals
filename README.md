1. Introduction
The food delivery industry has experienced rapid growth due to increasing internet usage and digital convenience. Mana Meals is developed as a frontend-based online food delivery application that provides users with a smooth and attractive food ordering experience. 9. Frontend Workflow
The frontend workflow follows a structured food ordering process.
Workflow Steps
Step 1
User opens the homepage.
Step 2
User browses restaurants or searches food items.
Step 3
User selects preferred food items.
Step 4
Selected items are added to the cart.
Step 5
User proceeds to checkout.
Step 6
Order confirmation screen is displayed.
The workflow is designed to reduce user effort and improve navigation efficiency.
